About

Hallcross Legal is an innovative law firm, providing exceptional legal services without the legalese.
At Hallcross Legal, we are dedicated to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients. Hallcross legal are specialists with a profound understanding of our key areas of law and have our clients' priorities and best interests at the forefront of our practice.

Our experienced lawyers have developed a reputation over their years of practice for being able to cut through the legalese and to provide our clients with succinct and straightforward advice, to achieve their desired outcomes.

Our philosophy is to provide our clients with exceptional advice and service, in a timely and cost-effective manner.
We believe in transparency in all that we do, so that your vision is also our vision, helping you achieve the outcomes you need to succeed.

Meet our Team

Nicholas Hallasso - Solicitor Director

Nicholas is a highly regarded solicitor that you want on your team. He is the founder and principal of Hallcross Legal and has in depth experience across all our areas of practice.
Nicholas has extensive experience acting for clients in all jurisdictions of the Australian Courts and frequents the Federal Court of Australia and the Supreme Court of New South Wales, together with other jurisdictions such as the District Court of New South Wales and the NCAT.
Nicholas has acted in a wide range of litigated and mediated disputes acting for a variety of private and public clients, across all aspects of the law.
Clients have described Nicholas as, rational, friendly, practical and easy to understand.
Nicholas founded Hallcross Legal to provide his clients with in depth legal advice, without the cost or the confusion that would usually come from engaging a law firm. Nicholas' ethos is to provide his clients with detailed legal advice, that is swift, succinct and straight to the point.
Prior to becoming a solicitor, Nicholas was a partner at a suburban accounting firm. His experience in both the law and his earlier practice as an accountant, enables him to provide simple yet tailored, in depth advice to his clients in all aspects of their business.
